JAMES ARTHUR

SONGS BIOGRAPHY

James Andrew Arthur is a British singer-songwriter from Middlesbrough, England. He rose to fame in 2012 after winning the ninth series of The X Factor. His debut single, "Impossible", was released after his win and it debuted at number one on the UK Singles Chart. This made him the first X Factor contestant in history to achieve this feat. Early Life and Career James Arthur was born in 1988 and grew up in Saltburn, North Yorkshire. He began playing guitar at age 13 and wrote songs soon after. After dropping out of college, he worked as a painter and decorator before entering the X Factor in 2012. The X Factor In 2012, James auditioned for The X Factor with a self-penned song titled "Impossible". His stunning performance earned him four yeses from the judges and he sailed through to the live shows.
